About the Artists

Andrew Edwards

Andrew has worked with many artists on many projects. He did the coloring on everything in this gallery.

Sterling Clark

The creator of the Renagade comics. Mr. Clark has worked with many of the biggest names in the comic book industry.

Leonard Pigg

Leonard Pigg is an all around artist. He works as a disc jockey in various clubs, has had a booth KokomoCon every year since it's incention, and has been a storyteller for years. There is not much this professional artist has not done.
